# 🍎 Apple Product Price Analysis using Python

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes an Apple product dataset containing **80,000 records** collected from **Amazon** and **Flipkart**. The analysis focuses on understanding pricing strategies, discounts, and price depreciation across various Apple product categories using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).

The project demonstrates data cleaning, statistical analysis, and visualization techniques to generate meaningful business insights from Apple product pricing data.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Compare Apple product prices across Amazon and Flipkart.
- Analyze discount trends for different Apple product categories.
- Identify Apple products with the largest price reductions.
- Examine the relationship between launch price and current selling price.
- Generate business insights using data visualization.

---

## ❓ Business Questions

1. Which platform offers Apple products at lower average prices?
2. Which Apple product categories receive the highest discounts?
3. Which Apple products have experienced the largest price drop since launch?
4. Is there a relationship between launch price and current selling price?
5. How do Amazon and Flipkart compare in pricing Apple products?

---

## 📌 Key Insights

- Amazon and Flipkart contain almost the same number of Apple product listings.
- Amazon's average current price is slightly higher than Flipkart's.
- Discount percentages differ across Apple product categories.
- Several Apple products have experienced significant price depreciation since launch.
- Launch price has a strong positive relationship with the current selling price.
